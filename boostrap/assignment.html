About KBN College

Kakaraparti Bhavanarayana College (Autonomous) is an exuberant outcome of a century old illustrious charitable organization, S.K.P.V.V. Hindu High Schools Society, which for the last 112 years, has been providing education through its eight reputable academic institutions.
It is one of the pioneering educational institutions in Vijayawada run with the motto
